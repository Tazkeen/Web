Our Story

Ajouhaar was founded in 2004 with the mission of providing the highest quality Halal meat to our community. Our dedication to ethical practices and top-notch service has been at the core of our business.

Ajouhaar Butchery is a trusted provider of high-quality Halal meat products, serving the local community with fresh, ethically sourced, and affordable meat. Committed to upholding the highest standards of cleanliness and quality, we ensure that all our products meet strict Halal guidelines. Our butchery not only serves individuals and families but also supports local businesses, restaurants, and events by supplying premium cuts of meat.

Beyond providing excellent service, Ajouhaar Butchery plays a vital role in the community by promoting healthy and sustainable food choices. We offer tailored meat packages for families, ensuring that everyone can enjoy nutritious meals at affordable prices. We also give back by donating meat products to local charities and organizations, especially during community events and religious celebrations, such as Ramadan and Eid. Our mission is to serve the community with integrity, foster strong relationships, and contribute to the well-being of our neighbors.
